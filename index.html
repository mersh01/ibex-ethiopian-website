Our Story

Where Tradition Meets Modern Elegance

At Ibex Ethiopian Bar & Restaurant, we bring the rich culinary heritage of Ethiopia to Columbus with a touch of modern sophistication. Our journey began with a passion for authentic Ethiopian flavors and a vision to create an unforgettable dining experience.

Every dish is crafted using traditional recipes passed down through generations, using the finest spices and ingredients. Our chefs combine time-honored techniques with contemporary presentation to create a dining experience that honors the past while embracing the present.

Whether you're here for a romantic dinner, family celebration, or business gathering, our warm hospitality and exquisite flavors will transport you to the heart of Ethiopia.
