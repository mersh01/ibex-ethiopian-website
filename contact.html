Frequently Asked Questions

Common questions about dining with us

Do I need a reservation?

Reservations are recommended, especially on weekends and for groups of 4 or more. Walk-ins are always welcome and we'll do our best to accommodate you based on availability.

Is there parking available?

Yes, Street parking is available on Shady Lane Rd.

Do you accommodate dietary restrictions?

Yes, we offer vegetarian, vegan, and gluten-free options. Please inform your server of any allergies or dietary restrictions when ordering.

Do you host private events?

Yes, we offer private dining for events, parties, and gatherings. Contact us directly for availability and pricing for groups of 10 or more.

What is the dress code?

We maintain a casual to smart casual dress code. While we welcome guests in comfortable attire, we ask that you avoid overly casual beachwear or athletic clothing.

Still Have Questions?

Feel free to call us directly during business hours.
